# THE ROAD — SALES PLAYBOOK
## SaaS SALES METHODOLOGY & PIPELINE PLAYBOOK

---

### 01 | LEAD QUALIFICATION (BANT Framework)
Before booking a private platform demonstration, every sales lead must be qualified against the following BANT criteria:

*   **Budget (الميزانية):** Does the salon have the capacity to invest in a premium SaaS solution? Multi-branch salons are prioritized.
*   **Authority (الصلاحية):** Is the lead contact the Salon Owner (المالكة), General Manager (المديرة), or a decision-making shareholder?
*   **Need (الاحتياج):** Is the salon facing operational leakages (e.g. commission errors, stock losses, or high appointment cancellations)?
*   **Timeline (الوقت):** Is the salon planning to launch or migrate system infrastructures within the next 30 to 60 days?

---

### 02 | PRE-QUALIFICATION DEMO SCRIPT OUTLINE
The presentation must follow a strict **"Hospitality before Software"** structure:

1.  **Welcome & Empathy (5 mins):** Welcome the lead. Acknowledge the unique operational beauty challenges in the GCC market.
2.  **Visual Showcase (15 mins):** Show the platform in action, emphasizing how it integrates into premium spaces. Show the tablet view first, highlighting the lack of clutter.
3.  **The Commission Solution (10 mins):** Walk through the tier-based stylist commission calculator, demonstrating how it eliminates math errors and saves manual manager time.
4.  **Security & Infrastructure (5 mins):** Highlight Odoo's world-class cloud data security and the localized K-Net API integration.
5.  **Interactive Q&A (10 mins):** Address questions without technical jargon, keeping terms simple and warm.

---

### 03 | ROI CALCULATOR FORMULAS
Demonstrate value by illustrating how The Road OS saves money:

#### 1. Revenue Protection Formula (Preventing Booking Leakage)
$$\text{Revenue Saved} = \text{No-Show Rate} \times \text{Avg. Ticket Price} \times \text{Bookings per Month} \times 0.85$$
*(Where 0.85 represents the success rate of our automated WhatsApp appointment confirmation reminders).*

#### 2. Staff Time Saved (Commissions Calculation)
$$\text{Hours Saved} = \text{Staff Count} \times 4 \text{ hours/month} \times \text{Manager Hourly Rate}$$
*(Automating tier-based commission calculations saves managers approximately 4 hours per stylist every month).*
