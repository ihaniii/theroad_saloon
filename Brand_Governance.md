# BRAND GOVERNANCE MANUAL 2026
## THE ROAD (الطريق) — MASTER EDITION

---

### 01 | BRAND PHILOSOPHY
The Road is not a software company; it is the silent, luxury operational partner for premium beauty and hospitality destinations in the GCC. 

*   **Who is The Road?** We are the invisible layer of technology that enables flawless hospitality. We serve salon owners, operators, and guests by transforming administrative friction into calm, synchronized workflow.
*   **Core Belief:** True luxury is effortless. Technology should serve hospitality, not distract from it. The guest should experience the harmony of the salon without ever seeing the system that coordinates it.
*   **Brand Sentiment:** After interacting with The Road, individuals should feel:
    *   **Calm:** Absolute operational control and clarity.
    *   **Confident:** Certain that all bookings, commissions, and products are perfectly aligned.
    *   **Valued:** Experiencing GCC hospitality tailored with respect, dignity, and warmth.

---

### 02 | VISUAL PRINCIPLES
Our visual design language is grounded in five core tenets:
1.  **Quiet Luxury:** No flashy components, loud buttons, or high-contrast drop-shadows. Quality is expressed through premium typography and vast whitespace.
2.  **Warm Hospitality:** The interface and photography must feel inviting, utilizing warm golden hour reflections, cream marble, and light oak tones.
3.  **Human Technology:** Technology is always shown inside a physical context (e.g., held naturally by a stylist or manager). No floating UI, exploded diagrams, or simulated dashboards.
4.  **Editorial Thinking:** Every digital screen, slide, or print catalog should read like an international design magazine. 
5.  **Timeless Design:** Avoid temporary visual trends (such as hyper-saturated gradients or extreme neo-brutalism). Rely on proportion and symmetry.

---

### 03 | LOGO RULES
The official logo of The Road is sacred and must never be altered, simplified, or redesigned.
*   **Safe Area:** A minimum clearance equal to 150% of the height of the logo emblem must be maintained on all sides.
*   **Minimum Size:** 
    *   **Print:** 15mm wide.
    *   **Screen:** 48px wide.
*   **Approved Colors:**
    *   **Brushed Brass / Gold Foil:** For architectural wall installations, packaging accents, and leather embossing.
    *   **Deep Teal (#142e34):** For white, cream, and warm beige backgrounds.
    *   **Soft White:** Only on solid Deep Teal backgrounds.
*   **Forbidden Usage:** Never rotate the logo, recolor it in primary colors, use outline versions, or place it on complex, non-approved photographic backgrounds.

---

### 04 | PHOTOGRAPHY RULES
All photography must belong to the same flagship salon environment defined in the *Photography Master Direction*.
*   **Approved Environments:** Arched structures, ivory marble surfaces, vertical fluted natural oak panels, champagne metal accents, and beige limestone floors.
*   **Approved Lighting:** Natural golden hour morning daylight, filtering through large floor-to-ceiling windows. Soft, indirect shelf lighting only.
*   **Approved Camera Heights:** Strict eye-level framing using 35mm and 50mm lenses for natural depth of field.
*   **Approved Wardrobe:** GCC-inspired uniforms in warm sand and cream tones. No synthetic materials or corporate business suits.
*   **Approved Emotions:** Calm focus, subtle smiles, and relaxed gestures. Strictly no direct eye contact with the camera.
*   **Forbidden Photography:** No cold corporate offices, dark backgrounds, high-contrast shadows, staging poses, or competitor equipment visible (e.g., Apple, Dyson, or L'Oréal logos).

---

### 05 | TYPOGRAPHY RULES
The typography hierarchy is designed to highlight the Arabic language as the primary hero.
*   **Arabic Headline Font:** **Amiri** (Serif). Used for main title blocks. Elegant, timeless, and large-scale.
*   **Arabic Body Font:** **Cairo** (Sans-serif). Used for all body text, bullet points, and interface text.
*   **English/Accents Font:** **Cormorant Garamond** (Serif). Reserved strictly for small, quiet metadata subtitles or page indicators.
*   **Paragraph Rules:** To ensure legibility, body copy must never exceed a **maximum width of 450px** or 60 characters per line.
*   **Line-Height:** Keep a generous line-height of **1.95** for Cairo and **1.35** for Amiri.

---

### 06 | COLOR RULES
The official palette draws inspiration from the natural materials of our physical spaces.
*   **Primary Palette:**
    *   **Deep Teal:** `#142e34` (Represents stability, trust, and depth).
    *   **Warm Beige:** `#faf7f2` (The primary background color for all digital and print layouts).
    *   **Ivory:** `#fafafa` (Used for containers and card backplates).
*   **Secondary Accent Palette:**
    *   **Rose Gold:** `#c88a82` (Used sparingly for active badges, indicators, and borders).
    *   **Champagne:** `#e5cdbc` (Used for dividers and metadata labels).
    *   **Oak Wood Tone:** `#c4a287`

---

### 07 | MATERIAL RULES
Every physical collateral and screen asset must reflect the official material library:
1.  **The Road Marble:** Warm ivory marble with thin, delicate veining.
2.  **The Road Oak:** Natural vertical fluted oak panels with a matte, non-glossy finish.
3.  **The Road Metals:** Brushed champagne metal and matte rose gold metal.
4.  **The Road Leather:** Soft-grain warm beige leather (used for welcome folders and portfolios).
5.  **The Road Fabrics:** Woven natural ivory linen.
6.  **The Road Glass:** Low-iron transparent glass.
7.  **The Road Stone:** Cream-toned textured limestone.

---

### 08 | PRODUCT RULES
Our product and packaging language is minimal, quiet, and premium.
*   **Approved Containers:** Matte soft-touch deep teal bottles, champagne metal caps, and rose gold accent rings.
*   **Label System:** Minimalist ivory labels with Arabic text occupying the central space, and a small English subtitle at the bottom.
*   **Forbidden Product Elements:** No high-gloss plastic bottles, neon colors, busy marketing claims, or crowded ingredient lists on the front labels.

---

### 09 | EDITORIAL RULES
*   **Whitespace:** A minimum of **35% of any page layout must remain entirely empty**. This whitespace represents confidence and calm.
*   **Rhythm:** Never repeat the same layout composition twice in a row. Alternate between full-bleed photos, split layouts, and text-only journal pages.
*   **Print Specs:** Optimized for landscape A4 (`297mm x 210mm`) with a 15mm safety margin on the right and left margins to accommodate physical bookbinding.

---

### 10 | DIGITAL RULES
*   **Dashboard Interfaces:** The Road OS dashboard on screen must remain minimal. Backgrounds must be warm beige, text must be deep teal, and data cells should occupy thin ivory containers. 
*   **Mobile App Layouts:** Rely on generous spacing between cards and elegant Amiri Arabic titles. Never use generic mobile templates.
*   **Social Media:** All layouts must use flat-lay photography, natural stone backdrops, and simple typography. No sales badges, discount banners, or marketing stickers.
