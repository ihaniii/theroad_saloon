# THE ROAD — IMPLEMENTATION PLAYBOOK
## 4-WEEK CUSTOMER ONBOARDING PATHway

---

### WEEK 1 | KICKOFF & SYSTEM CONFIGURATION

#### 01. KICKOFF MEETING (Day 1)
*   **Participants:** Customer Success Lead, Salon Owner, Salon General Manager.
*   **Goals:** Confirm the onboarding schedule, declare key user roles, and obtain current data files (staff rosters, client lists, product inventory).
*   **Outputs:** Kickoff document, shared project drive.

#### 02. SYSTEM PROVISIONING (Day 2 - 3)
*   **Tasks:** Initialize the client's dedicated cloud database instance.
*   **Branding Configuration:** Upload logo, map custom domain (`salonname.com`), configure base currency, and apply the customized Arabic system interface.

#### 03. DATA MIGRATION (Day 4 - 5)
*   **Tasks:** Clean and format raw client lists and product inventories. Import data into the database instance.
*   **Validation:** Cross-check active package balances and client history logs for consistency.

---

### WEEK 2 | CLIENT TESTING & TRAINING

#### 01. TRAIN-THE-TRAINER WORKSHOP (Day 6 - 8)
*   **Participants:** Customer Success Lead, Salon General Manager, Reception Lead.
*   **Goal:** Provide hands-on training on the calendar scheduling, inventory adjustments, and client CRM notes.
*   **Materials:** User Manuals, video guides from the Academy portal.

#### 02. STAFF ONBOARDING (Day 9 - 10)
*   **Tasks:** Register all salon stylists, technicians, and cashiers into the system database. 
*   **Roster Setup:** Configure work shifts, tier levels, and custom service commission percentage structures.

---

### WEEK 3 | THE PILOT PHASE

#### 01. DRY RUN (Day 11 - 12)
*   **Tasks:** Conduct simulated bookings and POS checkout transactions in a test environment.
*   **Goals:** Train staff on how to split payments (K-Net + Cash) and handle service package redemptions.

#### 02. LIVE PILOT (Day 13 - 15)
*   **Tasks:** Launch the system on a single styling chair or station for three days.
*   **Goals:** Gather immediate feedback, solve localized connectivity errors, and prepare for complete Go-Live.

---

### WEEK 4 | GO-LIVE & SYSTEM OPTIMIZATION

#### 01. COMPLETE GO-LIVE (Day 16 - 18)
*   **Tasks:** Transition all booking and cashier activities to the live database instance.
*   **Support:** A Customer Success Specialist remains on-site for the first 48 hours to assist with POS checkout and hardware terminal connectivity.

#### 02. OPTIMIZATION REVIEW (Day 19 - 20)
*   **Tasks:** Pull first weekly sales and staff commission reports.
*   **Goal:** Identify operational inefficiencies and optimize the staff interface layout based on initial feedback.
*   **Handoff:** Formally hand off the account to the long-term Customer Success Support team.
