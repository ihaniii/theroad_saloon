# THE ROAD — COMPANY OPERATING SYSTEM (COMPANY OS)
## VERSION 1.0 (EXECUTIVE BLUEPRINT)

---

### 01 | COMPANY STRUCTURE
The Road operates on a highly aligned, inter-departmental model. Below are the definitions, cadences, and metrics for each of the 10 core company departments.

---

### 02 | DEPARTMENTAL DIRECTIVES

#### 01. EXECUTIVE (CEO)
*   **Mission:** Steer company vision, raise capital, protect brand standards, and ensure long-term regional scaling.
*   **Responsibilities:** Board relations, major partnership signings, executive hiring, and strategic market expansion.
*   **KPIs:** Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), Net Revenue Retention (NRR), LTV/CAC ratio.
*   **Tools:** Notion, Slack, Slack-Huddle.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Leadership Alignment (Monday), Monthly Board Review.
*   **Escalation Path:** Final arbitrator for all financial, legal, and major brand compliance disputes.

#### 02. PRODUCT MANAGEMENT
*   **Mission:** Define and defend the Product Spec, prioritize the roadmap, and align features with GCC salon needs.
*   **Responsibilities:** Writing PRDs, coordinating user research, backlog grooming, and release coordination.
*   **KPIs:** Feature Adoption Rate, User Engagement, Time to Value (TTV).
*   **Tools:** Linear, Productboard, Amplitude.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Backlog Grooming, Monthly Roadmap Review.
*   **Escalation Path:** Disagreements regarding feature priorities are resolved between the Head of Product and CEO.

#### 03. ENGINEERING
*   **Mission:** Build and maintain scalable, secure, and fast platforms based on Odoo core systems.
*   **Responsibilities:** Code writing, system architecture, database security, server maintenance, and uptime stability.
*   **KPIs:** Platform Uptime (>99.9%), API Response Speeds (<200ms), Sprint velocity.
*   **Tools:** GitHub, Linear, New Relic, AWS.
*   **Cadence:** Daily Standup, Weekly Sprint Planning, Bi-Weekly Retrospective.
*   **Escalation Path:** Infrastructure or blocker issues escalate to the CTO, then to the CEO.

#### 04. DESIGN & ART DIRECTION
*   **Mission:** Protect the brand's visual identity and enforce the "Quiet Luxury" design system across all products.
*   **Responsibilities:** High-fidelity UI/UX design, customer journey illustrations, and marketing collateral design.
*   **KPIs:** Design QA pass rate, customer satisfaction (CSAT) with product interface.
*   **Tools:** Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud.
*   **Cadence:** Daily Design Review, Weekly Cross-functional Review.
*   **Escalation Path:** Design deviations are flagged by Art Direction and escalated directly to the CEO.

#### 05. CUSTOMER SUCCESS (CS)
*   **Mission:** Manage customer lifecycles, guarantee quick implementation, and drive software renewals.
*   **Responsibilities:** Managing customer onboarding, calculating customer health scores, and preventing churn.
*   **KPIs:** Gross Revenue Retention (GRR), Onboarding Time (<30 days), Customer Health Score.
*   **Tools:** Gainsight, HubSpot, Slack.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Customer Health Review, Monthly Success Audit.
*   **Escalation Path:** At-risk accounts are escalated to the Head of CS, then to the VP of Operations.

#### 06. SALES
*   **Mission:** Identify high-value GCC salons, demonstrate the platform value, and close enterprise deals.
*   **Responsibilities:** Lead qualification, pipeline management, client demonstrations, and contract negotiations.
*   **KPIs:** Sales Pipeline velocity, Close Rate, Average Contract Value (ACV).
*   **Tools:** HubSpot CRM, LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Pipeline Review, Monthly Commission Audit.
*   **Escalation Path:** Disagreements regarding custom pricing limits are escalated to the CEO.

#### 07. MARKETING
*   **Mission:** Drive brand awareness and generate qualified leads among GCC salon owners.
*   **Responsibilities:** Content creation, editorial newsletters, private event coordination, and SEO management.
*   **KPIs:** Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL), Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), SEO traffic.
*   **Tools:** HubSpot Marketing, Mailchimp, Webflow.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Marketing Alignment, Monthly Performance Review.
*   **Escalation Path:** Brand voice deviations are escalated to the CEO/Art Director.

#### 08. FINANCE
*   **Mission:** Manage company cash flow, coordinate taxation (VAT), and handle payroll calculations.
*   **Responsibilities:** Bookkeeping, VAT filing, payroll validation, and investor financial reporting.
*   **KPIs:** Monthly Cash Burn Rate, Runway (in months), VAT compliance accuracy.
*   **Tools:** Odoo Accounting, QuickBooks.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Cash Flow Review, Monthly Closing Audit.
*   **Escalation Path:** Payment blockages are escalated to the CEO.

#### 09. OPERATIONS
*   **Mission:** Coordinate physical resources, office spaces, and supplier relations.
*   **Responsibilities:** IT support for hardware, office leasing, and third-party vendor management.
*   **KPIs:** Vendor SLA compliance, operational expense ratio.
*   **Tools:** Notion, Slack, Jira Service Desk.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Operations Standup, Monthly Budget Audit.
*   **Escalation Path:** Supplier contract disputes escalate to the CEO.

#### 10. HR & RECRUITING
*   **Mission:** Attract top engineering, product, and customer success talent in the region.
*   **Responsibilities:** Recruiting, employee onboarding, career growth models, and team culture checks.
*   **KPIs:** Time to Hire, Employee Retention Rate, internal eNPS score.
*   **Tools:** Greenhouse, Notion.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly Hiring Standup, Monthly HR Review.
*   **Escalation Path:** Employee conflicts escalate directly to the CEO.
